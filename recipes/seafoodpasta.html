Creamy Seafood Pasta

How to Cook

Ingredients

  • 8 Large Tiger Prawn
  • 4 Large Scallops (Frozen)
  • 1 squid
  • 6 cloves of garlic
  • 280g of pasta
  • cooking wine
  • a stick of butter
  • half a cup of cream
  • Corriander
  • salt
  • pepper

Steps

  1. Deshell the prawns and keeps the head. Clean the squid and cut into pieces. Defrost the scallop in a bowl and keep the liquid that comes out from it.
  2. Fry the prawn shells with oil, then add water to the shells and let it boil for around 20 minutes to create a prawn broth
  3. Cook the pasta as per instructions. Save the pasta water
  4. Fry each seafood item individually with oil,salt and pepper. For all seafood, cook until sear appears.
  5. Add butter, garlic into the pan. Add cooking wine as well to deglaze the pan.
  6. Add scallop water,prawn broth and cream to the pan. Reduce until creamy, or add cornstarch to thicken.
  7. Add in the seafood and Corriander
  8. Add in Pasta and mix well before serving
