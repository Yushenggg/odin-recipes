Ginseng Chrysanthemum

How to Brew

Ingredients

  • 50g Dried Chrysanthemum
  • 30g American Ginseng
  • 2L Water
  • 60g Brown Sugar

Steps

  1. Add Chrysanthemum and Water to a pot and heat till boil. Let Chrysanthemum steep for 1 min then remove
  2. Lower the boil to a simmer and add the ginseng into the pot. Simmer for 5 minutes, then remove ginseng.
  3. Add sugar as you prefer.
